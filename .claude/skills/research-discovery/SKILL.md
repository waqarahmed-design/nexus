# Research & Discovery

UX research methods for understanding users, validating decisions, and synthesizing findings into actionable insights. Covers 10 user research methods and 5 synthesis frameworks.

## Method Selection Guide

Choose the right method based on what you need to learn:

| Question | Method |
|----------|--------|
| Why do users behave this way? | User Interviews, Ethnographic Research |
| Does this design work? | Usability Testing |
| What do users feel/think/do? | Empathy Mapping |
| What are users trying to accomplish? | Jobs-to-be-Done |
| Which version performs better? | A/B Testing |
| What do users think at scale? | Survey Design |
| What does the data show? | Analytics Interpretation |
| Who are our users? | Persona Development |
| Where do users struggle across the full journey? | Journey Mapping |
| What are competitors doing? | Competitive Analysis |
| What themes emerge from data? | Affinity Mapping |
| What does research mean for design? | Insight Generation |
| What does the business need? | Stakeholder Interviews |
| Does this interface follow usability principles? | Heuristic Evaluation |

## Research Phase Sequencing

```
Discovery (understand problem space)
  └── Stakeholder Interviews → User Interviews → Ethnographic Research

Definition (understand users)
  └── Persona Development → Empathy Mapping → Journey Mapping → Jobs-to-be-Done

Validation (test solutions)
  └── Usability Testing → A/B Testing → Survey Design

Measurement (track outcomes)
  └── Analytics Interpretation

Synthesis (at any phase)
  └── Affinity Mapping → Insight Generation → Heuristic Evaluation
```
